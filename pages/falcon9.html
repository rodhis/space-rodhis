The Falcon 9 is a two-stage reusable rocket designed and manufactured by SpaceX for the reliable and safe transportation of people and payloads to Earth orbit and beyond. Falcon 9 is the world's first orbital-class reusable rocket. The reusability allows the SpaceX reworks the most expensive parts of the rocket, which in turn reduces the cost of access to space.
