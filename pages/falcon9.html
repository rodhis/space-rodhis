Recovered rockets

The Falcon 9 is a two-stage reusable rocket designed and manufactured by SpaceX for the reliable and safe transportation of people and payloads to Earth orbit and beyond. Falcon 9 is the world's first orbital-class reusable rocket. The reusability allows the SpaceX reworks the most expensive parts of the rocket, which in turn reduces the cost of access to space. FALCON 9

FIRST STAGE

Falcon 9'S first stage incorporates nine Merlin engines and aluminum-lithium alloy tanks containing liquid oxygen and rocket-grade kerosene propellant (RP-1).

Falcon 9 generates more than 1.7 million pounds of thrust at sea level.

FALCON 9

SECOND STAGE

The second stage, powered by a single Merlin vacuum engine, delivers the Falcon 9 payload to the desired orbit. The second stage engine ignites a few seconds after stage separation and can be restarted multiple times to place various payloads into different orbits.

NUMBER OF ENGINES 1 vacuum
BURNING TIME 397 seg
THRUST 981 kN / 220.500 lbf

FALCON 9

INTERSTAGE

The interstate is a composite structure that connects the first and second stages and houses the pneumatic pushers that allow the first and second stages to separate during flight.

GRID FINS

The Falcon 9 is equipped with four hypersonic grid fins positioned at the base of the interstate. They guide the rocket during reentry by moving the center of pressure.

FALCON 9

PAYLOAD

Made from a carbon composite material, the fairing protects satellites on their way to orbit. The fairing launches approximately 3 minutes into flight, and SpaceX continues to recover the fairings for reuse on future missions.

HEIGHT 13.1m /43 ft
DIAMETER 5.2m /17.1 ft

FALCON 9

MERLIN

Merlin is a family of rocket engines developed by SpaceX for use in its Falcon 1, Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy launch vehicles. Merlin engines use rocket grade kerosene (RP-1) and liquid oxygen as rocket propellants in a gas generator power cycle. The Merlin engine was originally designed for salvage and reuse.

PROPELLANT LOX / RP-1
THRUST 845 kN / 190.000 lbf
